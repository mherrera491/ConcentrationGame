Vegas Card Flip

Welcome to Vegas Card Flip!

A simple game to test your memory. Here are some rules:

  • You will have 60 seconds to find all the match all of the cards
  • Press the "Start" button to shuffle the cards. You will have 10 seconds to look at the cards before they flip over.
  • Match all of teh cars under 60 seconds and you win!
  • If you need to restart for any reason, press the "Reset" button at any point during your play. Once you're ready to play again, press "Start"
